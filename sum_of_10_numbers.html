This page will calculate the sum of N numbers.

Problem statement: Get 10 numbers from the user, display the sum of the 10 numbers along with the numbers entered

Assumptions
  • User should be entering only numbers. No alphabets or special characters
  • User should be entering numbers in the range 1 to 1000
Pseudocode
  1. Accept the first number from the user
  2. Store the number
  3. Repeat steps 1&2, 10 times
  4. Initialise the sum to number 0
  5. Add first number to the sum
  6. Add the next number to the sum
  7. Repeat step 6 , 9 times
  8. Display the sum
  9. Display the numbers entered
